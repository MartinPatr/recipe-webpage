Recipe:Burger

Ingredients

  • Burger buns
  • Sauce(Mayo,Ketchup,hotsauce,lemon juice)
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato(with salt)
  • Onion
  • Cheese

How to make it

  1. Toast buns in butter
  2. Combine Sauce(little bit of hotsauce and lemon)
  3. Start putting the lettuce and tomato on bun(salt tomatos)
  4. Create ball out of beef and salt
  5. Add oil to pan and add beef ball(salt side down)
  6. With heavy object squish beef to make patty
  7. Add onion on top to cook with, then later add cheese
  8. Put everything together

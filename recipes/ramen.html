Recipe:Ramen

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Soy Sauce)
  • Garlic
  • Brown Sugar
  • Ramen noodles
  • Red Chili flakes
  • Egg
  • Everything Bagel Seasoning(Optional

How to make it

  1. Put butter and minced garlic(half a spoon) in pan
  2. Add soy sauce and mix
  3. Add spoon of brown sugar and drop ramen
  4. Add egg and red chili flakes
  5. Mix everything and add seasoning if want to

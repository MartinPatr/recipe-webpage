Recipe:Soup

Ingredients

  • 1 package wild and grain rice
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 2 cups chopped green pepper
  • 1 cup chopped onion
  • 1 jar (26 ounces) chunky tomato pasta sauce
  • 1 can (14-1/2 ounces) Italian diced tomatoes, un-drained
    • 1 can (14 ounces) beef broth

How to make it

  1. Prepare rice
  2. Cook beef,peppers and onion until meat is no longer pink
  3. Stir in the pasta sauce, tomatoes, broth and prepared rice

Recipe:Rice

Ingredients

  • Large Diced Onion
  • 1 Bell Pepper
  • Paprika
  • Tumeric
  • Chicken Broth
  • Rice

How to make it

  1. Add 1/2 Diced Onion
  2. Add 1 Bell Pepper diced
  3. Fry a bit (2-3 minutes)
  4. Add 1 tsp paprika and tumeric
  5. Cover(add water if it gets dry)
  6. 2 cups chicken broth
  7. 1 cup rice
  8. Allow it to boil for a bit(20 mins or until done)
